Built for simple, removable, real-piano playback.

The first version of PianoMate AI focuses on a clear promise: real acoustic piano sound, external installation, and AI-assisted music conversion.

Non-destructive setup

No permanent modification to the piano structure. Designed as an external system that can be placed and removed.

AI music conversion

Convert MP4, MP3, WAV, or MIDI sources into PianoMate playback data for real piano performance.

Real acoustic sound

Instead of speaker playback, PianoMate physically plays a real piano, preserving the instrument’s natural tone.

Phone control

Use a phone as the controller for playback and device interaction through Bluetooth connectivity.

Portable concept

The product concept includes accessories such as a movable trolley and a hard carrying case for easier handling.

For demos and homes

FAQ

Early answers for crowdfunding visitors.

Does PianoMate AI require piano modification?

The current concept is external and non-destructive. It is designed to sit on the piano without permanent structural modification.

Does it play through speakers?

No. PianoMate AI is designed to physically play the piano keys, so the sound comes from the real acoustic piano.

What music files can it use?

The current converter workflow is designed around MP4, MP3, WAV, and MIDI sources, with AI-assisted conversion into PianoMate playback data.

How heavy is the device?

The current prototype is approximately 11 kg. The weight helps keep the device stable during key actuation.
